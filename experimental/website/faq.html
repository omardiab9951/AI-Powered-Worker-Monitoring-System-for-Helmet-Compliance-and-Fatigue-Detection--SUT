What is the SUT dataset?

The SUT El Sewedy University DataSet contains images collected on campus for helmet compliance and fatigue detection. All data was captured and annotated by students.

How accurate is the helmet detector?

Accuracy depends on lighting and camera placement. Our prototype performed strongly in controlled tests but requires further validation in real-world conditions.

Can this run on CCTV/Edge?

Yes. The system is designed to run efficiently and can be deployed to edge devices or integrated with CCTV networks.

How to request a pilot?

You can use the Request Demo page or email projectHelmet@sut.edu.eg.
